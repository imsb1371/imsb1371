Built an image-based structural damage classification system using ensemble deep learning models. The approach combines multiple CNN architectures to improve reliability and outperform single-model baselines.

**Technical highlights:**
- Classifies damage types: flexural, shear, combined, and undamaged
- Ensemble of CNNs with averaging, stacking, and DE-optimized weighting
- Transfer learning with MobileNetV2 & NASNetMobile
- Significantly outperforms standard models (VGG, ResNet)
- Designed for rapid post-disaster structural assessment
